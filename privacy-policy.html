Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Daxus Template Monitor ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our WhatsApp Business integration application.

1. Information We Collect

Our application integrates with WhatsApp Business Platform to monitor message template status. We collect:

  • WhatsApp Business Account Information: Account ID, phone numbers, and business profile information
  • Message Template Data: Template names, status, quality ratings, and pause/approval information
  • Webhook Data: Events and notifications sent by Meta's WhatsApp Business API

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Monitor the status of your WhatsApp message templates
  • Send automated alerts when templates are paused, disabled, or approved
  • Provide notifications through configured channels (Slack, email, etc.)
  • Maintain the functionality and reliability of our monitoring service

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • Data is transmitted using secure HTTPS connections
  • We do not store message content or customer conversations
  • Template status information is only retained for operational purposes
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid legal requests
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property

5. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Meta WhatsApp Business API: For receiving template status updates
  • Slack (optional): For sending notifications
  • Email services (optional): For sending alerts

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Disconnect the application from your WhatsApp Business Account at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services. When you disconnect the application, we will delete your data within 30 days unless required to retain it for legal purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
